What is a Roadside Assistance (RSA) Program?

A Roadside Assistance (RSA) Program is a service offered by car insurance companies that provides support to vehicle owners in case of a breakdown on the road, including assistance like towing, flat tire replacement, battery jumpstart, fuel delivery, and sometimes even minor repairs, essentially helping to get the vehicle back on the road in an emergency situation; it's usually offered as an add-on feature to a comprehensive car insurance policy
