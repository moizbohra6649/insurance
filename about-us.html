About Company

Driving Innovation in Roadside Assistance and Spare Parts Retail

With years of experience in the automotive industry, RoadStar USA has established itself as a reliable provider of roadside assistance services and a trusted retailer of automotive spare parts. Our mission is to ensure that every driver has access to prompt assistance and quality parts whenever needed.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver prompt, professional roadside assistance services and offer a comprehensive selection of automotive spare parts through our user-friendly e-commerce platform. We strive to ensure that every driver has access to the support and products they need, whenever and wherever they need them.​

Who We Are

At RoadStar USA, we are dedicated to keeping drivers safe and vehicles running smoothly. With over 25 years of experience in the automotive industry, we have become a trusted name in providing reliable roadside assistance and high-quality automotive spare parts.​
